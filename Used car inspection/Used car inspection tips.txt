1)Test drive a cold engine
2)Test drive without music
3)Twist steering wheel to both extreme ends, notice any sound or difficulty
4)Go over bumps, notice any noise
5)Check braking bite with hard brake
6)Let go of steering wheel, vehicle should go straight
7)If wind noise without sunroof cover, then seal is worn off. If wind noise with sunroof cover, then even bigger worn out
8)Keeping car on with AC for a while 10+ mins
9)Turn on wipers, if not smooth, then blades worn out. If not going back into its position, then alignment or mechanism problem.
10)Horn, if dashboard lights dim, then battery is weak
11)Lock and unlock doors several times quickly. If fails, then the motor is weak
12)Same test with window
13)Same for ORVM
14)Check infotainment system and speakers
Use torch
15)Check battery date
16)Check engine oil leveler
17)Check if any drill holes in in engine bay, these are mods
18)Check for anything shinyer than others, this is a new part
19)Check from one side of the engine bay to the other side, so nothing is missed
20)Check for any leaked liquids arond caps like radiator cap, around pipe connections and clamps. Look for coolant, engine oil leaks etc
21)Check engine belt
22)Check alternator. Notice its oxidation on the outside, should match the engine oxidation. That means, its an original
23)Check for bite marks on pipes and clamps. These could be from rodents.
24)Check for weld marks, hammer marks on the chassis rails. If yes, then accident repairs
25)Check air filter
Advanced:
26)Check brake fluid. Remove the filter and access the fluid
27)Check spark plugs, if clean or carbon deposits
28)Check throttle body. Should be clean
29)Check bumper alignments
30)Check paint - need trained eye
31)Check for rust under the gaskets in doors, in the boot
32)Check spare tyre
33)Check if any moisture/condensation in headlights and breaklights
34)Check all nuts are present in the wheels
35)Check original marking on window glass, front and back and sides
36)Check cabin air filter
37)Check tyre age and tread
38)leaks and rust in suspension
39)Play in tyre. 
40)Spin the tyre. Any resistance or sounds
41)Exhaust tip for splutter and leaks
